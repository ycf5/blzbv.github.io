03. Select Treatment Units

Quiz Intro

Practice Selecting Treatment Units

The AB Treatments tool in Alteryx is designed to assist in identifying a representative set of treatment units from the pool of possible units.

In some cases, the treatment units are selected randomly, which is often the case with e-commerce. In this spa example, the treatment units were selected randomly.

Q1

What is the unit for this example?

SOLUTION: Store

Q2

What treatment groups will you have for this experiment?
SOLUTION: One treatment group for each price
