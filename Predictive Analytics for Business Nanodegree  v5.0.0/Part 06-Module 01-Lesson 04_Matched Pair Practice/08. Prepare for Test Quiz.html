Gathering Data

We have a great plan for our experiment, but before we get started, we need to think through a couple of items to ensure we gather the data we need.

Q1

Assuming a customer visits the spa about once every ten weeks, how long should our experiment last (how many weeks should we collect data)?
SOLUTION: 10

Q3

To use the A/B Analysis tool in Alteryx, we also need to ensure we have data available from a baseline period that we can compare to the experimental period to perform the analysis. Based on the duration of our experiment, how many weeks of data should we have for our comparison period?
SOLUTION: 10

Q2

We are going to use the A/B Trend tool in Alteryx to calculate trends and seasonality in our data. To do this, we need to make sure we have enough historical data available for the tool to use. How many weeks of historical data should we have?
SOLUTION: 64

Q4
