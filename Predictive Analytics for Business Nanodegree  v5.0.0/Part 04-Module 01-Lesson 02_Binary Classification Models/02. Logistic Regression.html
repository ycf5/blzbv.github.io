Intro

Introduction to Logistic Regression

Logistic regression is one of the most basic forms of regression modeling. It’s part of a family of “generalized linear models” or GLM for short. This basically means that the formula is very similar to that of a linear regression. However since the target variable is binary, instead of a continuous numeric variable, the target variable has to be modified to fit this GLM formula.
