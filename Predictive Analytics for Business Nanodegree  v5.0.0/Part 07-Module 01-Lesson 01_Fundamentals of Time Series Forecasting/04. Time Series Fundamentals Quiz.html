04. Time Series Fundamentals Quiz

Intro

Let's take a quick break to make sure you feel good about the basics. Answer the questions below.

Q1

A time series is a sequence of data points with the follow characteristics (choose all that apply):
SOLUTION:
  • Covers a continuous time interval
  • Equal spacing between every two consecutive measurements
  • Each time unit within the time interval has at most one data point

Q2

We would use time series forecasting to predict values for which following business situations (select all that apply):
SOLUTION:
  • Monthly beach bike rentals
  • A stock’s daily closing value
  • Annual sheep population
