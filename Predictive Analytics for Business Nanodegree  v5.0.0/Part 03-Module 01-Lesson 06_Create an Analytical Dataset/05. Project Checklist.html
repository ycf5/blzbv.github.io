Add browse tools to check your cleaning process. There might be an area where you are dropping parts of a value. Look at the original values and what they become by the end of the cleaning. Be careful of commas!

Outliers

Use the IQR method for each attribute (Census Population, Total Pawdacity Sales, Households with Under 18, Land Area, Population Density, Total Families) of the cities to see which cities have outlier values for any of these.
