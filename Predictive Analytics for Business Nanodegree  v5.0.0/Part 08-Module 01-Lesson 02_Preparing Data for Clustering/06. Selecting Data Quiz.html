06. Selecting Data Quiz

Selecting Data Quiz

Let’s take a look at three different objectives for segmenting as it could relate to customers. With each objective listed, select the variable that you think might have the LEAST value in creating the segments.

Question 1

Objective: Will use segments for marketing in order to enhance sales in a specific product category.
SOLUTION: Years of Loyalty program membership

Question 2

Objective: Determine customer profitability segments.
SOLUTION: Customer age

Question 2

Objective: Identify segments that have high potential for churn (i.e., taking their business elsewhere).
SOLUTION: Number of children
