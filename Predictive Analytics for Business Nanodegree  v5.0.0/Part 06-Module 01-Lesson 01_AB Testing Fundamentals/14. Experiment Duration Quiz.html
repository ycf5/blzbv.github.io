14. Experiment Duration Quiz

Experiment Duration

Determining Experiment Duration

For each of the following situations, identify what a cycle is, and think about how long that cycle would take in order to suggest how long an experiment should run.

Q1

A corporate recruiting company wants to determine if posting a job perk that let's employees work from home can decrease the time needed to fill the posted job.
SOLUTION: How long it takes from the time of posting a job until the job is filled

Q2

A farmer wants to know if planting a seed deeper will slow down the time to harvest his crops. What is a cycle for this experiment?
SOLUTION: How long it takes for the plant to grow enough to be ready for harvest
