07. Variables

Target Variable

Selecting the Target Variable

For each of the following, read the description of the experiment and choose what the target variable is for the experiment. Then list out a few possible control variables.

Q1

A corporate recruiting company wants to determine if they let their employees work from home, how will it affect their productivity?
SOLUTION: Productivity

Q1

QUESTION:

What are some possible control variables for the experiment above?

ANSWER:

A few control variables that I came up with are: tenure of the employee, gender, age, and marital status.

Q2

A farmer wants to know if planting a seed deeper will slow down the growth of his crops?
SOLUTION: Growth

Q2

QUESTION:

What are some possible control variables for the experiment above?

ANSWER:

A few control variables I came up with are: type of plant, slope of the ground, amount of direct sunlight, watering schedule, type of soil, and exposure to fertilizers.
