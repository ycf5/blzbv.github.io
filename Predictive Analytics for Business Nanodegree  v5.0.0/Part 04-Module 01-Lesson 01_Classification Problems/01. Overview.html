overview

Overview

In the previous courses, you've learned from Ben on how to choose predictor variables for your prediction models.

Ben will now explore with you a new class of predictive models: classification models. These models help identify what group a data point point belong to. For example, if we were to decide whether a person qualifies for a loan or not, we need to look at the person's loan application and classify whether the person should be in the approve loan group or the not approve loan group.

Ben will explore these classification models:

  1. Logistic Regression
  2. Decision Trees
  3. Random Forests
  4. Boosted Models

and walk you through an entire example of how to compare which model gives the best results for the given business problem in this course.
