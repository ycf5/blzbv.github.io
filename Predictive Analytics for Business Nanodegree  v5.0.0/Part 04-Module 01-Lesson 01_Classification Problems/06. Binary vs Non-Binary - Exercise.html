06. Binary vs Non-Binary - Exercise

Quizzes

Let's get a little practice distinguishing between binary and non-binary data. For each of the questions below, determine whether it involves binary or non-binary data.

Q1

Is an incoming email spam or should go into the inbox?
SOLUTION: Binary

Q2

Which color shirt looks best on me?
SOLUTION: Non-binary

Q3

What land cover type is a pixel from a satellite image?
SOLUTION: Non-binary
