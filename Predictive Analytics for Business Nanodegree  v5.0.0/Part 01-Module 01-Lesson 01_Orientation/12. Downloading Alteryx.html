12. Downloading Alteryx

Links to Download Alteryx

There are two links available to download Alteryx:

  1. Using File Download via Product Support page.

    1.1. Please download Alteryx Designer 2019.x with R-based Predictive Tools (advanced) for 64-bits (x refers to the latest minor). Note that Alteryx minor versions may change periodically.

    1.2 . Please use the Admin or Non-Admin installer based on your account permissions on your laptop. If using a corporate computer, you may need to request temporary Admin permission to install, please contact your IT team to submit that request.

  1. Sign in to the Alteryx Downloads and Licenses portal at licenses.alteryx.com.
    2.1. Create an account to download Alteryx.

2.2 Following this step, please choose Alteryx Designer 2019.x with R-based Predictive Tools (advanced) for 64-bit, where x refers to the minor version. Note that the versions can change periodically.

Start a Free Trial

  1. Once you get to the following screen: Download My Free Trial , fill out the Form to get a free trial and then click Start Now

If you are not currently working at a company and do not have the following details for the form:

Company
Business Email Address
Business Phone Number
You can use Udacity as the Company name. You can use the email address that you used for registration with Udacity for the business email address field. You can use your personal phone number for the business phone number field. click the version of the product you want to download:

Download Manager

  1. The file will then download, click on the downloaded file to run it. (you may need to double click it.)
    Then you need to select Alteryx Designer 2019.x with R-based Predictive Tools (advanced) for 64-bit, where x refers to the minor version. Note that the versions can change periodically.

Install

  1. Check the box to accept the license agreement and click Next. The software will begin installing. Click Ok to any windows that pop up or the predictive tools will not be installed.

Download complete

  1. Then click Next through the setup/installation steps for the predictive tools.

After it is done downloading the predictive tools you can click Finish to run Alteryx.

If you run into any issues please email us at preanalytics-support@udacity.com

NOTE: If you have any issues with activating your license please follow the offline license activation instructions here: https://community.alteryx.com/t5/Alteryx-Knowledge-Base/How-to-License-Alteryx/ta-p/140785
