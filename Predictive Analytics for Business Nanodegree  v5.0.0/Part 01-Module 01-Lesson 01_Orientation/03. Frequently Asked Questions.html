03. Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently asked questions specific to the Predictive Analytics for Business Nanodegree Program

What internet browser should I use?

We highly recommend using Google Chrome or Firefox, you may experience issues downloading files if you use Internet Explorer or Microsoft Edge

When I go to download files, I experience difficulties. How do I resolve this?

Please make sure you are using the latest version of Google Chrome. Please right click on your mouse and click "Save As…". From there you should be able to save your files with the format of your choice.

When will I get the Alteryx/Tableau license?

You will receive your Alteryx and Tableau licenses within a few business days of finishing your free trial. We send out licenses every Monday and Thursday.

How long are the Alteryx/Tableau licenses good for?

Both licenses last for 6 months, which should be plenty of time to finish the program. If the program takes you longer, you may request an extension as long as you’ve demonstrated significant progress in the program. The licenses are educational licenses, so they should not be used for commercial purposes.

What if I need technical help with Alteryx or Tableau?

Please use Student Hub or Knowledge for technical help. Other students have likely had the same questions. If you are unable to get answers, then email bizanalyst-support@udacity.com. Please do not go to the Alteryx or Tableau support websites except as a last resort.

Can I use other software besides Alteryx for my analysis?

You are welcome to use any software you wish, but keep in mind that we won’t be able to provide help with other tools outside the ones used in the Nanodegree program.

Do I need a Windows Operating system to take this program? Can I take this program if I have a Macintosh computer?

The Alteryx software requires a Windows operating system. You are welcome to use other analytical software, but it will not be supported in the program. If you have a Mac, this video explains what to do to run Windows on a Mac. So you can then install Alteryx on your computer.
